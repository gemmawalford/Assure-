Onboarding Path

1. Product Overview

Convene Assure is a governance and board evaluation platform that gives boards clear, evidence-based assurance – and a simple way to act when things need to improve.

What it is

A web-based governance and board evaluation tool that replaces scattered surveys, spreadsheets and PDFs with a single, structured view of board effectiveness, risk and compliance.

Who it's for

  • Housing associations & registered providers
  • Large charities and not-for-profit groups
  • Regulated organisations needing robust governance evidence

Top 3 Benefits

  1. Clarity – one view of governance strengths and gaps.
  2. Confidence – clear, repeatable evidence for regulators and funders.
  3. Follow-through – actions, owners and timelines tracked in one place.

30-second elevator pitch

“Convene Assure helps boards turn governance from a box-ticking exercise into a continuous improvement cycle — with structured evaluations, clear actions and a strong evidence trail for regulators and stakeholders.”

2. Ideal Customer & Use Cases

Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

  • Regulated, board-led organisations
  • Typically 8–20 board members plus committees
  • Existing pressure from regulators, funders or auditors
  • Board and governance teams stretched by manual processes

Buyer Personas

  • Economic buyer: CEO / CFO / Executive Director
  • Champion: Head of Governance / Company Secretary
  • Users: Board members, committee members, governance team

Core Use Cases

  • Annual or biennial board and committee evaluations.
  • Skills, diversity and succession mapping for the board.
  • Tracking progress against governance and regulatory actions.

Discovery questions

  • “How are you currently running board and committee evaluations?”
  • “What evidence do you use to demonstrate compliance to your regulator?”
  • “What’s hard or frustrating about your current governance processes?”

3. Talk Tracks & Demo Flow

Suggested call structure (30–45 mins)

  1. 3–5 mins: Rapport & agenda
  2. 10–15 mins: Discovery & qualification
  3. 15–20 mins: Tailored demo of Convene Assure
  4. 5 mins: Next steps and timeline

Opening talk track

“Thanks for making the time. I’d love to understand how you’re managing board and governance assurance today, share how peers are approaching it, and then show you how Convene Assure could strengthen your current approach.”

Demo storyline

  1. Start with the overview dashboard: “What’s working / what needs attention”.
  2. Drill into one evaluation: survey, responses and insights.
  3. Show the action tracker: owners, deadlines and progress.
  4. Finish with reporting: exports and evidence for regulators.
Tip: Anchor features in outcomes: fewer surprises for the regulator, clearer board conversations, less manual work for governance teams.

4. Common Objections & Responses

“We can just use surveys / Excel.”

Reframe: from tools to assurance.

“Surveys and spreadsheets work for one-off exercises, but they make it hard to track progress and demonstrate improvement over time. Convene Assure gives you a repeatable process and an evidence trail year-on-year.”

“We’re too busy this year.”

Reframe: low-lift implementation.

“Most clients are time-poor, so we handle the setup and configuration. Typically governance teams are live within a few weeks, working around board dates you already have.”

“Will board members actually use it?”

Reframe: simplicity and support.

“Board members receive a simple, secure link and can complete evaluations on any device. We also provide launch communications and support to make adoption easy.”
